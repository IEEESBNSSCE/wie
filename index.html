About Us

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is one of the largest international professional organizations dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists, and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests in a career in engineering.Our goal is to facilitate the recruitment and retention of women in technical disciplines globally. We envision a vibrant community of IEEE women and men collectively using their diverse talents to innovate for the benefit of humanity.

Why WiE

Women in Engineering is one of the largest international organisation that is dedicated for promoting women engineers and scientists, and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests in a career in engineering and science.WiE membership provides you an oppotunity to connect with professionals all around the world,volunteer for activities aimed for empowerment of women and girl students.IEEE WiE offers pre-university, undergraduate, and graduate student scholarship opportunities to learn from reputed universities all around the world.Become a part of WiE at our college and develop your skills to stand out as a true professional
